Over the past 40+ years we have built our Goalie Center to 3 full rooms. At WESCO professional coaches and players give expert advice and training techniques for all ages and levels of play. All of our staff is educated and well trained to properly fit and sell the newest goalie equipment from all the top equipment manufacturers including: Bauer®, Brians®, Brown®, CCM®, Eddy®, Graf®, Grit®, Reebok®, Sherwood®, Tackla®, TPS®, Vaughn®, Wall®, and Warrior®.


Along with all the newest and best goalie equipment on the market, we have our own state-of-the-art leg pad demo area, and a full training station to give all our goalies an edge over their competition. At WESCO we know goalies. We cater to the every need of every goalie, every day. We pride ourselves on providing the best customer service and expert knowledge of anyone in the area.
