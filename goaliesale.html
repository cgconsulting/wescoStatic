GOALIE DAY 2015

See below for more information regarding WESCO Sports Center's 2015 Annual Goalie Sale:


Sale Details:


WESCO Sports Center 2015 Annual Goalie Day Sale will take place Saturday July 18, 2015 - Monday July 20, 2015. The hours are as follows:
Saturday July 18, 2015 OPEN-10:00am CLOSE-6:00pm
Sunday July 19, 2015 OPEN-11:00am CLOSE-5:00pm
Monday July 20, 2015 OPEN-10:00am CLOSE 6:00pm
20% OFF will be given to all in stock goalie items. 10% OFF will be given to all out of stock items, orders, and custom orders. Discount is only available for purchases on one of the three sale days: July 18, 2015, July 19, 2015, and July 20, 2015. No discounts will be given prior to or after the three listed sale days. WESCO Sports Center will be fitting and holding items starting Monday July 13, 2015 through Friday July 17, 2015. Representatives from Reebok/CCM, Bauer Hockey, Vaughn, True, and Wall will be available on Saturday July 18, 2015, along with our own staff members and experts all weekend.
